CAT BREEDS
British Shorthair

British shorthair is one of the most famous breeds of cats in the world. They are medium to large sized cats that is fit for families.

Norwegian Forest Cat

A Norwegian Forest cat or 'Wegie' is gentle and friendly, great for first time cat owners.

Birman

Birman cats are good companions. They are friendly and relaxed, they love to be with people, and will generally get along with other cats and dogs.
